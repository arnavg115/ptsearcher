Formatting Fun

If you have any issues with formatting come here.

Names

If you are searching by names make sure spelling is correct and the first letter is capitalized and there are no spaces.

ExampleOxygen, Helium, Lead

Atomic Number

If your are searching by atomic numbers make sure you only enter the number.(Only elements from 1-109)

Example5,9,109

Atomic symbol

If your are searching by atomic numbers make sure you capitalize the first letter and the second is lower case and no spaces.

ExampleLi, Pb, Au
